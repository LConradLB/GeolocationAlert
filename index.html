QUICK START GUIDE

The app has two main elements, the first is the alarm & the second is the map.

Location Markers:
You can tap the map to place an location marker. Each marker responds to the radius that can be set on the main page - this is the radius around the marker. Once you enter the radius, the alarm will start playing and you phone will vibrate.

Alarm sounds:
You can set the sound of your alarm by taping on the selection box at the top of the screen.

Alarm buttons:
You can stop the alarm by pressing the dismiss alarm button. Once dismissed you can reset the alarm by tapping the reset button or exiting the radius of the marker.
